About me

Jana Uvarova

I am a UX/UI designer who codes, illustrates and simply loves all about technology. I constantly develop, learn new things and educate myself. For the past several years I've been working with startups helping them create beautiful products that delight users. I always strive to create simple, original, and impactful user experiences for web & mobile and urge for consistency across all platforms.

In 2020, I joined a full-stack coding bootcamp at Le Wagon Berlin to become more hands on with my designs. Straight after I jumped on a new challenge participating in the world's biggest Hackathon against the pandemic organized by German Government - #WIRVSVIRUS.

In my spare time I go to design meet ups, conferences and simply travel the world as it's the best mood booster.
